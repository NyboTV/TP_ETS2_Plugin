## 🚀 Features

Take full control of your rig with over **150+ real-time telemetry states** directly on your tablet:

- 🏎️ **Dynamic Visual Gauges**: High-performance rendering of your Speedometer, Tachometer (RPM), and Fuel levels.
- 🎨 **Fully Customizable**: Change the background, colors, fonts, and needle shapes of any gauge in real-time.
- 🗺️ **Job & Navigation Data**: See your current income (automatically converted to your local currency), destination, estimated time of arrival, and speed limits.
- 🚚 **Deep Truck Integration**: Monitor your engine wear, water/oil temperatures, gear, lights, and trailer cargo conditions.
- 🌐 **TruckersMP Support**: Check server populations, queues, and API status straight from your dash.
- ⚡ **Cross-Platform**: The plugin runs smoothly on Windows, Linux, and MacOS Touch Portal clients.
